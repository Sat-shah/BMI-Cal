Diet plays an important role on our daily intake of calories. Some of the food items, which may lead to weight gain include the following: Food rich in saturated fats · Food rich in sugar · Food rich in sodium · Alcohol and high calorie beverages

Consult a dietician to know about a specific diet plan. Some key components of a diet for weight loss may include the following: Food rich in protein · Food high in fibre · Healthy fruits and vegetables · Whole grains

Some of these diet plans are also known for helping to reduce weight: The Mediterranean diet · The Vegetarian diet · The Paleo diet

Eating a well balanced diet alone may not help in managing weight gain. It should be complemented with regular exercise.

Sedentary lifestyle involves having little or no physical activity. A person having a sedentary lifestyle burns less calorie, which leads to weight gain.

Tips for a more active lifestyle You can get started initially by just aiming to walk 30 mins/day. Once you get started with a more active lifestyle, you can take it a step further by adopting some of these exercises: Weight Training · Yoga · Cardio exercises Exercises alone may not help you in managing weight gain. It should be complemented with a well balanced diet.

People who are overweight are at an increased risk of many health conditions including the following: Hypertension · Type 2 Diabetes · Dyslipidemia · Coronary Heart Disease · Stroke · Osteoarthritis · Sleep apnea
